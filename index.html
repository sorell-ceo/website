Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Custo.

How do I get started with Custo?

Simply sign up, choose a plan, and start organizing your CRM in minutes, no setup required.

Is there a free trial available before subscribing?

Yes, Custo offers a free trial so you can explore all core features, test workflows, and decide if it fits your business, no credit card required.

Can I manage multiple pipelines at once?

Yes, Custo allows you to create and manage multiple custom pipelines and control over every stage of your sales process.

What types of businesses can use Custo?

Custo is flexible and scalable, making it ideal for startups, agencies, small businesses, and teams that need a streamlined way to manage customer data.

Can I import data from spreadsheets or another CRM?

Yes, importing is easy with our guided import tool, which supports CSV files and structured data from most CRM platforms with just a few simple steps.

How does Custo handle data backups?

We run automated backups on a regular schedule, storing data securely in the cloud to ensure your information stays protected and always recoverable.

Will I lose data if I downgrade my plan?

No worries, your data stays intact. Some features may be limited, but you’ll never lose important info when changing or downgrading your plan.

How often is Custo updated with new features?

Custo receives regular updates with new features, performance improvements, and security patches to ensure your CRM stays fast, secure, and reliable.
