Who I am

Not a developer.

I'm Anurag. 17. From Shimla. I have a chaotic brain that takes every "fun project" idea straight to startup level — and I've stopped fighting it.

Every build starts with something that has no business working on the setup I have. I attempt it anyway. I document the entire mess — the idea, the code, the part where everything breaks at 2am, and the moment it finally works.

Then I break down exactly how it works — no jargon, no gatekeeping. If I can figure this stuff out in Shimla on a potato laptop, the barrier is lower than you think.
